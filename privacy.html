Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Draft pending final legal review

This policy explains how Vamos Travel Korea ("Vamos a Jeju", "we", "us") handles the information you share through the Haenyeo Retreat website.

What we collect

When you submit an inquiry/application, we collect the details you provide: your name, email, nationality, chosen retreat and preferred date, number of guests, any dietary restrictions, accessibility or medical needs, and what you're hoping to find.

Why we collect it

  • To review your application and contact you about your retreat.
  • To plan your experience safely and comfortably (e.g. dietary, accessibility, or medical needs).

We do not sell your information or use it for advertising.

Sensitive information

Health, accessibility, and dietary details are used only to prepare for your retreat. By submitting them, you consent to our using them for this purpose. You may ask us to delete them at any time.

How it's processed

Form submissions are delivered through our form provider, Formspree, and reach us by email and in the Formspree dashboard. We keep your information only as long as needed to handle your inquiry and booking.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the information we hold about you. Just email us and we'll take care of it.

Contact

Questions about your data? Email soraya@vamosajeju.com.

This is a working draft for the inquiry stage. A full policy — including our registered business details and booking/payment data handling — will be published when online payments open in 2026.
